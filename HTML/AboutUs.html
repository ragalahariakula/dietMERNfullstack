About Us

Established in 2009, Dhanekula Inst Of Engineering Technology is a premier Institute located in Andhra Pradesh. Institute offers Degree and Diploma courses including 7 UG and 3 PG courses. Additionally, it also has After 10th Diploma courses. These programs are offerred in Full Time mode. Dhanekula Inst Of Engineering Technology is a premier institute for M.E./M.Tech, B.E. / B.Tech, After 1th Diploma courses. Candidate can pursue in multiple range of courses like M.E./M.Tech, B.E. / B.Tech, After 1th Diploma with the stream of Engineering. Over the period, Dhanekula Inst Of Engineering Technology has gained expertize through its trained and experienced faculty in the fields of VLSI Design, Information Technology, Computer Science Engineering, Civil Engineering, Electrical Engineering, Electronics & Communication Engineering, Mechanical Engineering, Environmental Engineering. Courses offered by institute are recognized by approving bodies like AICTE. Dhanekula Inst Of Engineering Technology offered courses for 984 seats. Courses offered by the Institute are in the fee range of INR 70,000-243,200 for a total of 984 candidates.
